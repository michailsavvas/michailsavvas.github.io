I am an Assistant Professor at the Mathematics Department of the University of Iowa.
Previously, I held postdoctoral positions at the University of Texas at Austin and the University of California, San Diego.

I completed my Ph.D. at the Mathematics Department of Stanford University in 2018 under the guidance of Professor Jun Li. Before that, I completed my undergraduate studies at Trinity College, University of Cambridge in 2012.

My main research interests span enumerative geometry, moduli theory, Donaldson-Thomas theory, derived algebraic & differential geometry, derived categories & stability conditions, logarithmic geometry, and artificial intelligence.
